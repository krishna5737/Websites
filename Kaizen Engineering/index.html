Welcome to Kaizen

In an age where the importance of Low carbon emission and clean energy is gradually gaining momentum, Kaizen Engineering has been a pioneer of clean energy through end to end Gas installation services and related products. We provide turnkey solutions for Gas Installation to various companies in sectors ranging from Hospitality, Healthcare to automation and Information Technology. We carry out installations as per Oil Industry safety Directorate (OISD 162), IS 6044/2013, Gas Cylinder Rules 2004/2012, Controller of Explosives (PESO), Department of Industrial Safety and Health (DISH) Factory Act Rule No.13.

We offer products and services from gas pipeline installation, custom-made equipment, and burners for a specific use. “Any Gas, gas related services; You Name it, We Have it!”
